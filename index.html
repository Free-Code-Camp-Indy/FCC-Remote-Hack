1st Free Code Camp Remote Hackathon

Details

When: May 6, 2017

Duration: 24-hours

Theme: Games

Team Size: 3-5 people per team

Winner: Receives a certificate and satisfaction :)

Rules

  1. Projects must be started during the event, not before.
  2. Judges will be neutral and not affiliated with any FCC group participating.
  3. Code must be posted and not updated after code freeze at the end of the hackathon.
  4. Be nice and respectful of other teams, judges, and participants.
  5. Have fun!
